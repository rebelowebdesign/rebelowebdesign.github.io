About Me

It all started in 2011. A game that I played, Habbo Hotel, had an active community of developers that were trying to emulate a "retro" version of the official game, using PHP, CSS, HTML, JS as the web stack and C# for the emulator server.

It was such a huge community, eventually I wanted to also host my own custom Habbo hotel website too. I did it, but had no idea what I was doing at the time. I didn't know what source code was, i thought I was just modifying "stuff for a game", as broad as that sounds. I had no idea what a programming language, or any of that was. But from there, I began.

Fast forward to 2017. With years of experience and learning, I finally begin to see real progress in my journey. Starting many side projects for fun, learning new technologies along the way, and diving into the world of Freelancing with Wordpress and PHP.

In 2024, I finally felt ready to fully commit to my skills and proudly perform freelance services under my last name; Rebelo Web Design.
