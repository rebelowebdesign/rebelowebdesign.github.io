Background

MY
STORY

It all started in 2011. A game I played — Habbo Hotel — had an active developer community trying to emulate a "retro" version using PHP, CSS, HTML, JS and C# for the server. I jumped in, had no idea what I was doing, but from there, I began.

Fast forward to 2017. Years of experience crystallized into real progress — side projects, new technologies, diving into freelancing with WordPress and PHP.

In 2024, I finally felt ready to fully commit to my craft and proudly launch freelance services under my name: Rebelo Web Design.
