INTRODUCTION

This Privacy Policy explains how Rebelo Web Design, operated by Kristopher Rebelo ("I", "me", or "my"), handles information in connection with this website located at rebelowebdesign.com.

I take your privacy seriously. This site is a personal portfolio and does not engage in e-commerce, advertising, or data brokering of any kind. This policy is intended to be transparent and straightforward.

01 — DATA COLLECTION

INFORMATION I COLLECT

This website does not collect personal data automatically. I do not run analytics software, tracking pixels, or advertising networks on this site.

The only information I may receive is what you voluntarily provide when you choose to contact me directly, such as:

I do not control the data practices of these third parties and encourage you to review their respective policies.

You can manage or disable cookies at any time through your browser settings. Disabling third-party cookies will not affect your ability to use this site.
